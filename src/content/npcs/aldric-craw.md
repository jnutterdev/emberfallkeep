Aldric Craw has run the Stormreach harbour for twenty-three years. He knows every ship that comes and goes, every captain worth trusting, and most of the secrets that pass through on the tide.

He owes the party a favour after they pulled him out of a difficult situation in Session 1 — he's repaid this by providing them with passage records and a safe berth, but he's careful not to appear too friendly in public.
