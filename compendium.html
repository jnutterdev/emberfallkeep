The Iron Covenant

Military Order

Hostile

A militant order that believes the Shattered Crown must be reassembled to restore order to Valdris — by any means necessary. Their agents are everywhere the party has been.

"Order through strength. Strength through unity."

Notable: Rook (captured), Three unnamed agents (deceased)

The Dawnsworn

Religious Order

Allied

Keepers of the old seals. They have guarded the first shard for three centuries and are beginning to doubt whether their mandate to protect it still holds.

"What is sealed shall remain sealed."

Notable: Halveth Duskmore, Brother Edwick

House Maerath

Noble House

Neutral

One of three surviving noble houses. Playing all sides with practiced patience. Their interest in the crown is dynastic, not religious — whoever holds it rules Valdris.

"We endure."

Notable: Lady Thessira Maerath, Tavin Maerath

Thornwall City Guard

Municipal Military

Unknown

Ostensibly neutral city law enforcement. Someone within their ranks is reporting the party's movements to an unknown party. Captain Hollan may not be aware.
