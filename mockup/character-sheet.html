Backstory

Korrath Ironveil served ten years as a sergeant in the Valdris Crown Legion before the wars shattered both the crown and the army he'd given his youth to. He was not a survivor in the lucky sense — he made himself one, dragging three wounded soldiers out of the Ashenveil Pass before the Covenant's forces sealed it.

He doesn't talk about the soldiers he couldn't carry.

After the Legion's dissolution, Korrath drifted into contract work. Not because he wanted to, but because standing still left too much room for memory. The escort job that started this campaign was supposed to be simple. Three days. Good pay. No one shooting at them.

He finds it bitterly funny that Ashenveil is where it all circled back to.

Notes

Korrath recognised the maker's mark on the longsword recovered in session XVII. He has not told the party. When Halveth reacted to the mark, Korrath watched him very carefully.

He is beginning to suspect that Caelindra's custody of both shards is a mistake, but does not know how to raise this without breaking the party's trust in her.
