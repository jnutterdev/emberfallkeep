The Story So Far

The war-torn kingdom of Valdris has gone silent. Three factions vie for the pieces of a crown that should not exist — and the party stands at the centre of it all, unaware that the shards are already calling to one of their own.

What began as a simple escort contract through the Ashenveil Pass has spiralled into a conspiracy that reaches back three centuries to a king who should have stayed dead. The Iron Covenant wants the crown reassembled. The Dawnsworn want it destroyed. And House Maerath simply wants to be the one holding it when the dust settles.

Current Situation

Following the events in Ashenveil, the party has recovered the second shard and returned it to Caelindra's custody — a decision that is beginning to show cracks. Halveth's amulet, now confirmed as a binding seal, is responding to the shard in ways none of the party fully understand.

A missive from Thornwall arrived the morning after the funeral pyres burned out. Someone in that city knows they were in Ashenveil. Someone was watching.
